# Anonymization Guide

Use this guide when creating public examples, case studies, or shareable summaries from client-call transcripts.

## Naming Convention

- Replace real companies with `Company 1 - [industry role]`.
- Replace people with `Client A - [role]`.
- Replace internal team members with `Team Member 1 - [role]`.
- Keep seniority and business function when useful.
- Remove overly specific domain details that could identify the client.

Examples:

- `Company 1 - contractor`
- `Company 2 - professional services firm`
- `Company 3 - prefab/manufacturing company`
- `Company 4 - developer`
- `Client A - Designer`
- `Client B - Owner`
- `Client C - CEO`
- `Client D - Project Manager`
- `Team Member 1 - PM`
- `Team Member 2 - CEO`

## What to Remove or Generalize

Remove or replace:

- real company names,
- real person names,
- exact job titles that reveal a small organization,
- exact addresses,
- exact project names,
- exact city names,
- exact URLs,
- exact email addresses or phone numbers,
- unique product names,
- raw transcript excerpts,
- rare domain facts that could identify the company.

Use replacements:

- `City 1`
- `Region 1`
- `Project Site 1`
- `our platform`
- `the product`
- `the vendor`
- `generic AI tools`
- `manual research`
- `client-facing deliverable`

## Role Simplification

Keep role utility but remove identifying detail.

| Too specific | Public version |
|---|---|
| `project manager / technical-financial evaluator for a narrow project type` | `Client G - Project Manager` |
| `land acquisition or screening workflow stakeholder` | `Client H - Project Manager` |
| `sales/preconstruction lead` | `Client C - Decision Maker` |
| `secretary, spouse of executive` | `Client I - Administrative Staff` |
| `general manager, runs several companies` | `Client E - General Manager` |

## Quote Handling

- Avoid raw transcript excerpts in public examples.
- If a quote is necessary, keep it short and remove identifying details.
- Prefer paraphrase over quotation.
- Do not quote unusual phrasing that could be searchable or personally identifying.

## Public Demo Checklist

Before publishing, scan for:

- real company names,
- real person names,
- project names,
- exact locations,
- internal product names,
- raw transcript excerpts,
- over-specific titles,
- sensitive pricing or contract terms,
- legal, regulatory, financial, or technical guarantees.

The final public example should show the method and reasoning, not the client's identity.
